Autism Assessments

Autism assessments are available for children and adults. We recommend referrals are only made after consultation with your doctor or another health professional to assist in identifying traits suggestive of an Autism diagnosis.

For child clients, a parent/caregiver interview is first conducted, followed by an individual assessment. While the child is with the psychologist/assessor, the parent/caregiver will be asked to complete additional assessment tools online if not completed prior.

The cost of $2300 includes:

  • Autism assessment (ADOS)
  • Clinical interview in line with the ADI-R
  • Assessment of function
  • A comprehensive diagnostic report
  • Additional questionnaires tailored to the client as needed
  • A letter with statement of diagnosis
  • Feedback session (optional)

Our assessments are completed by Psychologists. NDIS require a consensus team diagnosis for children over 9 and adults to access the scheme. This means an additional health professional will need to ratify the diagnosis (e.g. a Paediatrician, Speech Pathologist, Psychiatrist). Children accessing NDIS under the Early Childhood Early Intervention (ECEI: under 9 years old) pathway do not require a formal diagnosis for initial access, however need to work towards obtaining one in order to stay on the scheme long-term.

The Department of Education have different requirements to the NDIS. Sign off from a ‘Lead Practitioner’ such as a Paediatrician or Psychiatrist is required.

*We follow the Autism CRC and meet criteria for participating in diagnosis as part of a consensus team. Whilst we do our best to stay up to date with the various institutional requirements, we encourage all clients to ensure that our assessments will meet their needs by checking with the relevant institutions (NDIS/Dept. of Ed) and clearly communicating their goals for accessing assessment with us so we can ensure we are the right fit*

ADHD Assessments

ADHD assessments are available for children aged 6+ and adults. We recommend referrals are only made after consultation with your doctor or another health professional to assist in identifying traits suggestive of an ADHD diagnosis.

For children, a parent interview is conducted followed by an observation of the child in our office space and a cognitive assessment. On some occasions, it may be appropriate for the child to participate in the interview. Questionnaires will be sent to the clients parent/caregiver and teacher where applicable. School reports and supporting documentation will also be requested.

Adult and/or Teen assessments are completed in one appointment in our office, consisting of the interview and cognitive assessment. Questionnaires will also be sent to the client and a secondary person known to them.

The cost of $1600 includes:

  • Clinical interview
  • ADHD assessment
  • Assessment of function
  • Cognitive Assessment
  • Observation(s) where relevant
  • Comprehensive diagnostic report
  • Additional questionnaires tailored to the client as needed
  • A letter with statement of diagnosis
  • Feedback session (optional)

Please be aware that psychologists cannot prescribe or make recommendations about medication. Medication is prescribed by Paediatrician’s and Psychiatrists. If you’re seeking pharmaceutical intervention, please consult with a Paediatrician or Psychiatrist for this.

Cognitive Assessments

Cognitive assessments are available for children 6+ and adults. We recommend referrals are only made after consultation with your school, doctor or another health professional to assist in identifying the need for a cognitive assessment.

For children, a parent interview is conducted followed by an observation of the child in our office space and a cognitive assessment. On some occasions, it may be appropriate for the child to participate in the interview. Questionnaires will be sent to the clients parent/caregiver and teacher where applicable. School reports and supporting documentation will also be requested.

Adult and/or Teen assessments are completed in one appointment in our office, consisting of the interview and cognitive assessment. Questionnaires will also be sent to the client and a secondary person known to them.

The cost of $1100 includes:

  • Wechsler Intelligence Scale for Children (WISC) OR
  • Wechsler Adult Intelligence Scale for Adults (WAIS)
  • Clinical interview
  • Assessment of function
  • A comprehensive diagnostic report
  • Additional questionnaires tailored to the client as needed
  • A letter with statement of diagnosis
  • Feedback session (optional)
