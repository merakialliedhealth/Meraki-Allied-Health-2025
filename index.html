About Meraki

Meraki Allied Health is a small company with a focus on providing individualised services to our clients, their families and caregivers.

With a special interest in working with children, we service Rockingham, Mandurah, and the Eastern suburbs (Forrestfield and surrounds). We provide community based services, meaning we can travel to where you need us! We also have an office in Secret Harbour for those who prefer to come to us. We conduct our assess services in secret harbour and high wycombe

We currently provide Psychology, Play Based Therapy, Positive Behaviour Support and Autism, ADHD and Cognitive assessments.

At Meraki, we implement a Neuro-affirming model to all therapeutic services. This means we use a strengths-based approach focusing on the goals of the client, whilst embracing their uniqueness.

Our services

Play based Therapy

Our dedicated therapists draw from the Child Centred Play Therapy (CCPT) framework and principles to provide play based therapies to children. Play based therapies supports children learn about themselves and their world. When children feel sad, worried or troubled about an event or incident they may not be able to talk about their feelings like adults do - instead, children communicate through their play.

Play based therapy for children is a professional therapeutic process that uses play as the medium for the child to “talk” about their feelings, much like an adult does when they see a psychologist. Play therapy tools are specifically selected toys and play materials which facilitate play communication so that the child may easily manipulate them in their own way and at their own pace.

Positive Behaviour Support

Positive Behaviour Support is designed to support clients, their family members and caregivers to make environmental changes that enable the individual to build upon their strengths and ensure their needs are met.

PBS services that we provide include:

  • Behaviour Support Assessment and Functional Behaviour Assessment
  • Behaviour Support Plan development
  • Behaviour Support Plan Implementation, including training for parents, caregivers and stakeholders
  • Letters of recommendation.

Psychology

Meraki provides Psychological counselling to children and their caregivers, adolescents, and adults. Our dedicated therapists will work alongside you to identify individual goals, and support you in achieving them. All services are tailored to the needs of the client and focus on the concerns presented by the individual.

Psychology services we provide include:

Assessments
