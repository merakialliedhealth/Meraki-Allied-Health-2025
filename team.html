Courtney Patterson

I am a Registered Psychologist and Behaviour Support Practitioner with a passion for working with children and their families. I have worked in the disability sector for over eight years, allowing me to gain experience in various different techniques and strategies. I strongly believe it takes a village to raise a child and enjoy working with parents, teachers, and caregivers as a unit to support young people.

As much as adults use verbal language to communicate, children use play, which is why I aim to ensure all of my sessions are playful and adapted to the client’s interests. I believe therapy doesn’t have to be formal to be therapeutic. My areas of interest include Child Centred Play Therapy (CCPT), diagnostic assessments, and the Polyvagal Theory.

I service Secret Harbour and surrounds, providing NDIS services (Psychology & BSP), play therapy, privately funded services, and assessments (ASD & ADHD).

Nicola Wollington

I am a Social Worker and certified Play Therapist with over 20 years of experience supporting children, young people and their families. I am experienced in supporting families with trauma, family separation, anxiety, neurodivergence, emotional regulation challenges, school issues and social challenges.

In addition to Child-Centred Play Therapy, I am a certified Autplay therapist and have training in Synergetic Play Therapy, which is a research-informed model that combines the therapeutic power of play with nervous system regulation, interpersonal neurobiology, physics, attachment, mindfulness, and therapist authenticity. I am passionate about utilising children's natural language of play to foster their emotional well-being, healing, and growth.

My approach is person-centred, neuro-affirming, trauma-informed, attachment focussed, playful and empathic. I understand the joys and challenges of parenting and recognise that each parent's journey is unique. I value collaboration with parents, schools, and other professionals.

I service Secret Harbour on Thursdays, and Gosnells and surrounds on Mondays & Fridays.
